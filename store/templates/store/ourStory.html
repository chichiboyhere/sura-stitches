Our Success Story

Sura Stitches

Sura Stitches was founded on the 17th of March, 2018. The dream was to make a brand that represents the true African essence ‐ strong, colourful and bold. And we haven't looked back ever since

Our team of dedicated staff and workers at our Lagos head-quarters and five other branches across the a country, ensure that our customers have nothing but praise for the service they get.

Our international partners include the Key Textiles, Ghana, Hengli Group, China, Yunsa, Turkey

Our Vision

To spread the beauty of African attire to the world.

Our Mission

Every one deserves a touch of African beauty, enwrapped in elegant frabrics. We want to see to it that happens.
